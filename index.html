About

At Sweet Aroma, we believe in the power of a beautifully lit space and the calming effect of a luscious scent. Founded with a passion for creating deliciously-scented eco-friendly candles, our mission is to handcraft each candle with the highest quality materials.


Made with lead-free cotton wicks, 100% soy wax and premium fragrance oils, our candles fill your room with the warmth and comfort of a clean, long-lasting burn.


Whether you’re looking to create a cozy atmosphere or gift your loved ones something special, our candles are the perfect addition to your space. Let us bring light, love and relaxation to your home, one candle at a time.

F.A.Q.s

What ingredients are used in your candles?

Our candles are made with 100% soy wax, which is eco-friendly and burns cleanly. Our wicks are made from lead-free cotton, and we use high-quality fragrance oils that are phthalate-free.

How long do your candles burn?

Burn time depends on the size of the candle. Our standard candles burn for 40-50 hours, while our larger candles can burn for up to 80 hours.

Are your candles safe to use around pets and children?

Yes! Our candles are made from non-toxic ingredients and burn cleanly. However, we strongly recommend always supervising children and pets around any open flame.

What's the best way to burn my new candle?

Trim the wick to about ¼ inch before each use to prevent excess soot. Let the candle burn long enough for the top layer of wax to melt evenly across the surface, avoiding tunneling.

Do you offer gift sets?

Yes! We have a variety of beautifully packaged gift sets that make perfect presents for special occasions such as weddings, corporate gifts, and more. Contact us for more information on creating a personalized candle experience.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this to check the status of your delivery.

What if my candle arrives broken or damaged?

We take great care in packaging our candles, but if your candle arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order, and we will happily replace it or issue a refund.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs vary depending on the destination. You can calculate shipping at checkout or contact us for more information.
